Events

I create unforgettable experiences events that leave guests begging for the next invite.

THE DAILY FRONT ROW X NICOLE MILLER ROSÉ BRUNCH AT BOUNCE BEACH MONTAUK

Nicole Miller partnered with the Daily Front Row to promote her latest fragrance at Bounce Beach in Montauk, where guests enjoyed Nicole Miller Rose inspired frozen cocktails. Guests were treated to an exquisite brunch with the ocean waves breaking in the distance. Personalized perfume bottles were give aways for the guest to commemorate the fantastic afternoon. The event resulted in an increased awareness and sales for Nicole Miller fragrances.

MOMMY AND ME DENIM EVENT

Nicole Miller threw a super fun carnival-style Mommy and Me event that brought moms and kids together for a day of excitement. The duos jazzed up with matching Nicole Miller jackets, became the characters of their choice with colorful faces paintings and ate more cookies and brownies than you’ll find in a bakery on a Sunday morning. My highlight…..Unveiling and getting everyone excited about the cool new Mommy and Me denim collection. The result was a fantastic day full of smiles, shared moments, and a buzz that spread the love for Nicole Miller's stylish and family-friendly vibe.

ELEMENTS BARRE FIT

Nicole Miller joined forces with Elements Barre Fit for a dynamic collaboration to showcase the latest in Nicole Miller activewear. The lively and enjoyable Barre class was a hit, with everyone looking fantastic in their Nicole Miller activewear sipping on Nicole Miller Rose. The event didn't just stop at the workout – it led to a wave of successful influencer posts featuring Nicole Miller activewear and increased sales. It was a win-win celebration of fitness, fashion, and a touch of rosé.

UJA SUMMERFEST FT AMY SHUMER

Summerfest exceeded all expectations, raising over $1.6 million at its annual benefit concert headlined by the hilarious Amy Schumer. Over 1,200 philanthropic Long Islanders gathered under a sprawling tent outside the NYCB Theater in Westbury. The event was a hit with abundant food, free-flowing drinks, and an infectious festive spirit that had everyone celebrating in style.

Norwegian Cruise Line

The Norwegian Cruise Line's "Why Not" campaign’s goal was to transform entertainment for 18-20-year-olds. My role included conducting surveys, analyzing trends, and leading focus groups to guide the development of entertainment programs. Through concept testing sessions, we evaluated the effectiveness and appeal of tailored experiences for this demographic. The campaign's success marked a shift in cruise ship entertainment, promising a memorable and vibrant experience for young adults.

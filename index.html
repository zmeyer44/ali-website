THE DAILY FRONT ROW X NICOLE MILLER ROSÉ BRUNCH AT BOUNCE BEACH MONTAUK

Nicole Miller collaborated with the Daily Front Row to launch her latest fragrance at Bounce Beach in Montauk. Guests enjoyed Nicole Miller Rose-inspired frozen cocktails during an exquisite brunch, with the backdrop of ocean waves breaking in the distance. I initiated influencer outreach, organized fittings, dressed guests, and devised a comprehensive social media strategy to promote the event. Additionally, I conceptualized various activations including perfume bottle engraving and cocktail making. I packed the gift bags and decorated the event space. This event not only amplified awareness but also drove increased sales for Nicole Miller fragrances.

UJA SUMMERFEST FT AMY SHUMER

Summerfest exceeded all expectations, raising over $1.6 million at its annual benefit concert headlined by the hilarious Amy Schumer. Over 1,200 philanthropic Long Islanders gathered under a sprawling tent outside the NYCB Theater in Westbury. The event was a hit with abundant food, free-flowing drinks, and an infectious festive spirit that had everyone celebrating in style. In my role, I oversaw the entire guest journey from the moment of ticket purchase to the end of the event. I meticulously planned every detail, including organizing raffles, developing detailed descriptions of the prizes, and utilizing the Giftsmart platform to manage the process. I ensured that name tags were stuffed, seating charts developed and chairs were labeled accordingly. This attention to detail contributed to the seamless execution of the event, enhancing the overall experience for all attendees..

ELEMENTS BARRE FIT

Nicole Miller joined forces with Elements Barre Fit for a dynamic collaboration to showcase the latest in Nicole Miller activewear. The lively and enjoyable Barre class was a hit, with everyone looking fantastic in their Nicole Miller activewear sipping on Nicole Miller Rose. The event didn't just stop at the workout – it led to a wave of successful influencer posts featuring Nicole Miller activewear and increased sales. In my role I composed the guest list, styled influencers, and orchestrated event setup, including product merchandising and décor. Additionally, I captured compelling event content and devised a strategic social media strategy, amplifying the event's impact and engagement. It was a win-win celebration of fitness, fashion, and a touch of rosé.

MOMMY AND ME DENIM EVENT

Nicole Miller threw a super fun carnival-style Mommy and Me event that brought moms and kids together for a day of excitement. The duos jazzed up with matching Nicole Miller jackets, became the characters of their choice with colorful face paintings, and ate more cookies and brownies than you’ll find in a bakery on a Sunday morning.I played a pivotal role in the event setup, ensuring all elements were in place to enhance the overall experience for attendees. I warmly welcomed guests upon their arrival to the showroom, ensuring both parent and child completed the necessary photo release forms. Throughout the event, I collected creative content and assisted children with various activities. My highlight…..Unveiling and getting everyone excited about the cool new Mommy and Me denim collection. The result was a fantastic day full of smiles, shared moments, and a buzz that spread the love for Nicole Miller's stylish and family-friendly vibe.
